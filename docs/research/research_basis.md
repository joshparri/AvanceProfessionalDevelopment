## Product implication
The app should:
- use public company information only as a starting reference
- allow Josh to mark items as verified / unverified
- remain useful even when exact internal workflows evolve
- support manual notes more than brittle integrations

## Important safety assumptions

- personal notes may include operational observations but should avoid unsafe storage of sensitive client data
